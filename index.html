Welcome Home.

We’re building a parklet to connect Washington Square Park and Grant St. for the many North Beach inhabitants, visitors, and dwellers. It’s a great place to catch some shade under the trees, meet with people, or get a little work done. Designed by top-ranked Columbia Architecture graduates and master fabricators, our Parklet will be a highlight in San Francisco.

One parklet, infinite possibilities.

We’re starting small, but together there’s nothing we cannot do!

Banter

Grab a seat and meet some of the locals in the neighborhood you often see walking around. You’ll love the interesting banter and backgrounds of North Beach’s finest.

Connect

Connect Washington Square Park to Grant St. so our many visitors see the whole neighborhood and pick up a few things from the great shops around.

Work

Need a change of scenery from your (home) office? Come grab a seat and use the speedy and freely available WIFI made possible by HITW Coffee.

Caffeinate

Enjoy a morning, afternoon, or evening coffee (or coconut) in the parklet, proudly served by HITW Coffee - a locally run and operated coffee shop born in North Beach.

Participate

Learn about North Beach from our community board and get involved with local events in the neighborhood.

Timeline

How we will get there.

  • Q3 2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    We were inspired by other great parklets around the city and felt we could add a useful piece to our neighborhood for locals and visitors alike.

  • Q4 2016

    A Group Forms

    A small, volunteer, team is formed to design and build the parklet pending neighborhood and city approval.

  • Q1 2017

    Getting Official

    With a (hopefully) approved parklet application by city agencies we will raise funds to build our North Beach parklet.

  • Q2 2017

    Build and Open

    We open a wonderful parklet to our community and grow its use in new and interesting ways for a long time.
